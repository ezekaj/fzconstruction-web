About Corner Building

Corner Building is a modern commercial development located in a prime business district of Tirana. The building offers a mix of retail and office spaces, designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes.

The ground floor features retail spaces with large storefront windows, ideal for shops, restaurants, and service businesses. The upper floors offer flexible office spaces that can be customized to suit your business requirements.

With its strategic corner location, the building offers excellent visibility and accessibility, making it an ideal investment opportunity for businesses looking to establish a presence in Tirana.

Key Features:

  • Prime corner location
  • Modern architectural design
  • Flexible retail and office spaces
  • Energy-efficient building systems
  • Secure underground parking
  • 24/7 security and maintenance
  • High-speed internet infrastructure
  • Accessibility features
