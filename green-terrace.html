About Green Terrace Complex

Green Terrace Complex is a modern residential development located in one of the most desirable areas of Tirana. The complex offers a perfect blend of urban living and natural surroundings, with extensive green spaces and premium amenities.

The complex features a variety of apartment layouts, from cozy one-bedroom units to spacious three-bedroom family homes. Each apartment is designed with attention to detail, featuring high-quality finishes, modern appliances, and energy-efficient systems.

Residents of Green Terrace Complex enjoy access to a range of amenities, including a swimming pool, fitness center, children's playground, and 24/7 security. The underground parking ensures convenience and safety for all residents.

Key Features:

  • Modern architectural design
  • High-quality construction materials
  • Energy-efficient building systems
  • Spacious balconies and terraces
  • Landscaped gardens and green spaces
  • Community facilities and amenities
  • Secure underground parking
  • 24/7 security and maintenance
