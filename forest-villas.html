About Forest Villas

Forest Villas is an exclusive luxury villa complex nestled in a serene natural setting on the outskirts of Tirana. Each villa is designed to provide the perfect balance of luxury, comfort, and harmony with nature.

The complex features a collection of individually designed villas, each with its own private garden, swimming pool, and stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The villas range from 3 to 5 bedrooms, offering ample space for families and entertaining guests.

With a focus on sustainable living, the villas incorporate eco-friendly features such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting systems, and energy-efficient appliances. The complex also includes a communal clubhouse, tennis courts, and walking trails.
